Hi, I'm Haneen

I sit at an interesting junction in life. I’m a lawyer turned relationship & intimacy coach. I feel polarised all the time! On one hand, I’m Haneen - the studious law geek with years of nerding on legal dilemmas, contracts and client negotiation. On the other hand, I’m Haneen - the crazy curly haired dancer who loves hiking, talking about relationships and teaches intimacy through embodied coaching! So I do both.

Shortly after I qualified as a solicitor in 2018, I hopped into the startup world launching sensual immersive theatre. It was then that I realised I could combine my legal ninja skills for entrepreneurs who might bury their head in the sand with all things legal. Let’s face it, it can be a complicated and daunting area for most of us.

Anyway. Long story short - I decided to be my own boss and actually help people fulfilling their passions, to get their legal necessities done, in a way that feels super easy and fun! This feels far more aligned to me, because I have been in both worlds and I’m mindful that lawyers are too expensive to be accessible to startups.

*Disclaimer:
Although I am a qualified solicitor (you can check me on the Solicitors Regulation Authority), I am a non-practicing solicitor. This means I am not regulated by the SRA; I am simply guiding you as a mentor through legal matters and not acting in the capacity of a solicitor. If you need help with very complex legal matters, or you want 100% peace of mind & watertight cover because the stakes are high, I am not the person for you. I am happy to recommend solicitors who could help however! Please note I only work on matters within the jurisdiction of England & Wales.
